About Us

DiskSpaceX envisions a decentralized file storage system that revolutionizes data management through a seamless integration of blockchain technology, encryption, and distributed storage. At its core, the system empowers users with a secure and intuitive interface to upload, encrypt, and retrieve their data, ensuring privacy and protection. The encryption module fortifies data security, enabling files to be stored in encrypted form across distributed storage units, providing redundancy and scalability.

    A robust blockchain network orchestrates operations by leveraging smart contracts to automate and securely manage storage transactions. The use of a Ganache node facilitates seamless contract execution and testing, while sharding enhances scalability by dividing the blockchain into smaller shards for parallel transaction processing, significantly improving throughput and efficiency.

DiskSpaceX is designed to eliminate dependency on centralized storage providers by leveraging decentralized storage systems such as IPFS, ensuring transparency, reliability, and resilience.
